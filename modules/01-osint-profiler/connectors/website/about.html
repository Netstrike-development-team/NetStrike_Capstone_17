About SimCorp Financial

More than three decades of building trusted relationships with Canadian institutions — grounded in rigorous analysis, disciplined execution, and unwavering fiduciary duty.

Our Story

Built in Ottawa. Trusted Across Canada.

SimCorp Financial Services was founded in 1987 by a group of former Bank of Canada economists who believed that mid-market corporate clients deserved the same calibre of investment management previously available only to pension funds and sovereign wealth vehicles.

From a single floor at 150 Elgin Street, the firm has grown to 340 employees across three offices in Ottawa, Toronto, and Vancouver. Today, SimCorp manages over $14.2 billion in assets under management on behalf of 420+ institutional and corporate clients.

In 2019 we received OSFI registration as a federally regulated financial institution, and completed ISO 27001 certification in 2021 — underscoring the seriousness with which we approach security, data governance, and operational resilience.

Our mission: To be the most trusted steward of institutional capital in Canada by combining quantitative rigour with client-centric service.

Milestones
1987
Founded in Ottawa

Five founding partners establish SimCorp Capital Management on Elgin Street with $40M in seed mandates from three Ontario credit unions.

1995
Toronto Expansion

Opened Bay Street office; AUM crosses $1B milestone. Launched the proprietary SimScore risk-rating methodology.

2003
Rebranded to SimCorp Financial Services

Expanded beyond equities into fixed income and alternative assets. Became a CIRO member dealer.

2011
Vancouver Office Opens

West Coast presence established to serve BC public sector clients and growing technology sector mandates.

2019
OSFI Federal Registration

Became a federally regulated financial institution, extending our mandate to serve federally-chartered clients.

2021
ISO 27001 Certification

Zero non-conformities on first audit; implemented enterprise SIEM, EDR on all endpoints, and MFA rollout across all systems.

2024
Okta IAM Platform Deployment

Full migration of all staff to Okta identity and access management, replacing legacy ADFS. Single sign-on now active across 40+ internal applications at simcorp.okta.com.
