Frequently Asked

Quick Answers

  • I can't log into my employee account. Who do I call?
    Contact the IT Helpdesk at +1-613-555-0100 or email helpdesk@simcorp.com. For after-hours emergencies use +1-613-555-0101. You can also reset your password via the self-service portal at simcorp.okta.com.
  • How do I apply for a position at SimCorp?
    Visit our Careers page to see open roles, or send your CV directly to recruiting@simcorp.com with the role title in the subject line.
  • I'm a journalist — who handles media enquiries?
    Please email media@simcorp.com or contact the Executive Office at +1-613-555-0199. We aim to respond to all media enquiries within 24 hours on business days.
  • How do I report a suspicious email or security concern?
    Forward suspicious emails to security@simcorp.com or call the Security Operations line at +1-613-555-0120. Do not click any links in suspicious messages.
  • I need to file a complaint about SimCorp services.
    Please write to compliance@simcorp.com with details of your concern. All complaints are acknowledged within 2 business days and escalated to our Client Ombudsman if unresolved within 5 days.
