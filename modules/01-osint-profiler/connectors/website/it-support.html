Password Reset Procedure

  1. Navigate to the Okta Portal

    Open a browser and go to simcorp.okta.com. Click "Forgot Password?" below the login form.

  2. Verify Your Identity

    Enter your SimCorp email address (e.g., firstname.lastname@simcorp.com) and confirm via the Okta Verify push notification on your registered device.

  3. Choose a New Password

    Passwords must be at least 16 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a symbol. Passwords expire every 90 days.

  4. MFA Device Not Available?

    Call the IT Helpdesk at +1-613-555-0100 with your employee ID. After-hours: +1-613-555-0101. Identity verification required — please have your employee badge number ready.

New Employee Onboarding

  1. Receive Your Welcome Email

    HR will send an Okta activation link to your personal email 48 hours before your start date. The link expires in 7 days.

  2. Activate Your Okta Account

    Follow the link, set your password, and enrol your mobile device with the Okta Verify app. This is your master login for all SimCorp systems.

  3. Install Required Software

    Once logged in to simcorp.okta.com, navigate to the App Catalogue to install Cisco AnyConnect, Microsoft 365, and any department-specific tools. IT can pre-provision laptops on request — email helpdesk@simcorp.com.

  4. Connect to VPN

    Open Cisco AnyConnect and enter vpn.simcorp.com as the server. Authenticate with your Okta credentials. VPN is required for all access to internal file shares, the trading platform, and compliance systems.
