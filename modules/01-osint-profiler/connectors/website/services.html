Our Services

From portfolio construction to regulatory reporting — SimCorp delivers institutional-grade financial services across six integrated practice areas.

01 — Core Service

Investment Management

Discretionary and advisory mandates across fixed income, Canadian and global equities, real assets, and select alternative strategies. Our portfolio team applies the proprietary SimScore risk-rating framework to every holding, balancing return objectives against liquidity and regulatory constraints.

All mandates are managed from our Ottawa headquarters with real-time oversight through BlackRock Aladdin OMS and Bloomberg B-PIPE market data feeds.

  • Fixed income: government, corporate, and high-yield
  • Canadian and global equity (active and passive overlays)
  • Real estate investment trust allocation
  • Alternative risk premia and liquid alternatives
  • Custom liability-driven investment frameworks
  • CIRO-compliant order management and best-execution reporting

Financial Advisory

Strategic counsel for corporate treasuries, boards, and pension sponsors navigating capital allocation decisions, M&A considerations, and treasury policy review. 03 — Operations

Asset Servicing

End-to-end middle- and back-office outsourcing for asset managers and institutional investors. SimCorp provides custody coordination, fund administration, trade settlement, NAV calculation, and CIRO-compliant regulatory reporting — removing operational burden from lean investment teams.

  • Fund administration and NAV calculation (daily)
  • Custody and subcustody coordination
  • Trade confirmation, matching, and settlement
  • Corporate actions processing and reconciliation
  • OSFI and NI 81-102 regulatory reporting
  • Investor reporting and statement generation

04 — Technology

Digital Banking & API Integration

For corporate treasury teams needing real-time multi-bank visibility and automated cash management. SimCorp's digital banking layer connects via ISO 20022-compliant APIs to major Canadian clearing institutions, providing a consolidated view of intraday liquidity and automated sweep execution.

  • Multi-bank connectivity (RBC, TD, BMO, Scotiabank, CIBC)
  • Intraday liquidity monitoring and reporting
  • Automated cash concentration and zero-balance accounts
  • FX execution and hedging workflow integration
  • SWIFT MT/MX and ISO 20022 message processing
